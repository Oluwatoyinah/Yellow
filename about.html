About Us

Affordable and well-constructed homes in Nigeria

We believe that Nigerians in Diaspora deserve affordable and well-constructed homes in Nigeria, which could serve as an investment vehicles or as a place to live, play and relax in. This is why Fortezza homes is leading the way by offering properties developed with the highest standards in construction, which are smart, energy efficient and highly secure. We have collaborated with highly reputable and trustworthy property developers in the country who are known for delivering the best in real estate. We have also partnered with forward-looking financial institutions to provide financing to your home and land acquisition and building-construction needs.
We aim to serve you and we strive for your utmost satisfaction whilst working with us.

Our services

Sale and short-let of residential property
At Fortezza homes, we offer an array of luxurious and affordable residential properties for sale and short-let across the country, all constructed by the most reliable real estate developers in the country. We are particular about matching our clients with the bests homes which suit their requirements and preferences. We also work hand in hand with our trusted property developers to ensure that residential properties still under construction are tailored to suit your demands.

Land and new home acquisition :
clients interested in acquiring land property and residential properties outside of our offerings are able to consult us to act on your behalf during the process of negotiation and acquisition and even through the construction and completion process, whilst always acting solely in our client’s best interest and confidence, ensuring their peace of mind at all times.

Acquisition of property financing and mortgage facilities:
we have collaborated with a reputable financial institution to provide our clients with attractive payment plans in the form of mortgage facilities, at affordable interest rates, allowing for their purchase of our offered properties as well as properties which may be under construction by our clients.

Property management:

we provide property management services for our clients who might be too busy to do it on their own, who are out of the country, or need professional help. For clients who look to maximize returns on their real estate investment, we advise on and provide a range of services to ensure that their properties are heightened in value and generating an influx of revenue even in their absence.
